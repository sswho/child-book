Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of photo should I upload?

For best results, upload a clear, well-lit photo showing your child's face from the front. While our AI works with most photos, close-up shots without sunglasses or costumes work best. Don't worry‚Äîwe'll help you evaluate your photo during the process.

How personalized are the stories?

Each story is uniquely generated based on your child's name, interests, and age. You can choose from adventure themes like space exploration, undersea discovery, dinosaur adventures, fairy tales, and many more. The AI creates a completely original narrative featuring your child as the hero.

What age range are these books appropriate for?

Our books are designed for children ages 2-8, with content and complexity adjusted based on the age you specify. The durable board book format is perfect for toddlers, while the engaging stories captivate older children too.

How quickly will I receive my book?

After you approve your preview, most books arrive within 3 business days (US addresses). International shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. If your book doesn't arrive on time, we'll refund your shipping costs.

What if I'm not happy with my book?

We stand by our Double Happiness Guarantee. If you or your child aren't completely satisfied with your book for any reason, we'll either reprint it with adjustments or provide a full refund‚Äîyour choice. Simply contact us within 30 days of receiving your book.
